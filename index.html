How to Play

Build your dream Masters team within budget and compete for the top prize.

1

Pick Five Golfers

Select exactly five golfers from the field of 86 players competing at Augusta National.

2

Stay Under Budget

The total cost of your five golfers cannot exceed $20.00. Costs are based on FanDuel Sportsbook odds.

3

Multiple Entries Allowed

You may submit more than one team. Each entry costs $20 via e-transfer.

4

Entry Fee & Deadline

$20 per team via e-transfer to charles.joyce@me.com (reference: First Name, Last Name - Masters). Entries due by 8:30 AM on Thursday, April 9.

5

Scoring

Final standings are based on the sum of tournament prize money earned by the five golfers on your team.

6

Submit Your Team

Use the Team Builder tab to pick your squad, then save it. Head to My Teams to review and officially submit.

Cloud sync active. All entrants will see everyone's teams and standings in real time.
Note: Cloud sync requires the page to be hosted on a web server (not opened directly as a file). Upload to tiiny.host or Netlify to enable sync.
